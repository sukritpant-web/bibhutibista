Our Mission

At Bibhuti Bista - Research and Consulting Services, our mission is to support organizations, policymakers, and other stakeholders by providing expert evaluation, studies, and research services that are methodologically sound, data-driven, and tailored to the client's needs. We believe in a tailored approach, ensuring that our methodologies are not only rigorous and data-driven but also specifically designed to address our clients' unique needs and challenges.

Our services encompass a wide range of research methods and evaluation techniques, allowing us to deliver actionable insights and evidence-based recommendations that drive effective decision-making and foster positive outcomes for our communities. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and integrity in all our services, fostering a collaborative environment that encourages innovation and continuous improvement.

Our Vision

The vision of individual evaluation and research consultants is to establish them as a reliable and strategic partner for organizations committed to maximizing their impact and enhancing their outcomes. This is achieved through the delivery of thorough, evidence-based assessments and in-depth research that provide valuable insights and practical recommendations tailored to each organization's unique requirements.

We aim to instill a robust culture of data-driven decision-making, encouraging organizations to leverage data and evidence in their strategic planning and operational processes. By fostering an environment of continuous improvement, Bibhuti Bista Research and Consulting Services seek to empower organizations across various sectors. Ultimately, these collaborative efforts contribute to meaningful positive change in social, economic, and organizational contexts, leading to better outcomes for individuals and communities.

The Journey

Understanding the Development Sector and the Actors

The journey in the development sector started back in 2005 from an organization called Spiny Babbler. Implementing community development projects, working closely with the community was the embarking moment in this sector. This initial experience was transformative, as it deepened his understanding of the challenges communities face and highlighted the importance of participatory approaches in ensuring sustainable change. The journey continued till the end of 2011 in different capacities in different Non-Governmental Organizations in Nepal and university in Finland as a researcher.

Following my tenure at Spiny Babbler, he continued to expand his knowledge and impact in the development sector until the end of 2010. He then took on various roles within different non-governmental organizations (NGOs) in Nepal, where he engaged in project management, administration, monitoring and evaluation, capacity building, and community outreach. Each position allowed him to refine his skills and adapt to each organization's unique contexts and challenges. These projects involved working directly with local communities to identify their needs and empower them to drive their own development.

Bibhuti collaborated closely with various stakeholders throughout this journey, including government counterparts, bilateral organizations, Universities, and donors. These partnerships were essential in fostering a multifaceted approach to development, as they facilitated resource sharing and strengthened the overall impact of initiatives. By working together, our collective expertise ensured that the projects were aligned with the broader goals of the MDG (2015) and the SDG (2030).

World of Evaluation and Research

In 2012, Bibhuti had the incredible opportunity to work alongside Dr. Dil Prasad Shrestha, a highly respected expert in monitoring and evaluation (M&E). During this time, Bibhuti engaged in various assignments that allowed him to apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings. Under Dr. Shrestha's mentorship, he received invaluable guidance that helped him navigate the complexities of evaluation methodologies and frameworks.

Dr. Dil's constant support boosted Bibhuti's confidence and encouraged him to think critically and creatively about qualitative and quantitative analysis and interpretation. As Bibhuti delved deeper into different projects, he gained hands-on experience designing evaluation frameworks, collecting and analyzing data, and presenting findings to stakeholders.

This collaborative experience was enlightening and immensely fulfilling, as it allowed him to contribute to meaningful research and evaluation efforts. The milestones Bibhuti achieved during this period significantly enhanced his understanding of the evaluation landscape and solidified his passion for research. Ultimately, this journey set the stage for Bibhuti's continued growth and exploration in evaluation and research.
